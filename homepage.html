2018 Summer Semester

The Long Beach Japanese Language School is offering a new summer session. This summer's theme is Japanese Gardens. Some of the events during the summer are:
  • A traditional tea ceremony
  • Calligraphy
  • Haiku Class
  • and more!
Classes are every Saturday starting July 7 to August 18, 8:45 a.m. to 12:30 p.m.
Space is limited so sign up early!
